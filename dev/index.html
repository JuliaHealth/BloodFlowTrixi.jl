BloodFlowTrixi.BloodFlowEquations1D — Type
BloodFlowEquations1D(;h,rho=1.0,xi=0.25)

Blood Flow equations in one space dimension. The equations are given by

\[\left\{\begin{aligned} \frac{\partial a}{\partial t} + \frac{\partial}{\partial x}(Q) &= 0 \\ \frac{\partial Q}{\partial t} + \frac{\partial}{\partial x}\left(\frac{Q^2}{a+A_0} + (a+A_0) P(a)\right) &= P(a) \frac{\partial}{\partial x}\left(a+A_0\right) - 2 \pi \sqrt{\frac{a+A_0}{\pi}} k \frac Q {a+A_0}\\ P(a) &= P_{ext} + \frac{Eh\sqrt{\pi}}{1-\xi^2}\frac{\sqrt{a+A_0} - \sqrt{A_0}}{A_0} \end{aligned}\right.\]
